# 🌍 My Travel Planner AI

An intelligent, full-stack travel itinerary planner that crafts highly personalized day-by-day travel guides, realistic budget breakdowns, curated hotel recommendations, and adaptive packing checklists. Powered by **Gemini AI**, it automatically checks and flags geographically unrealistic transport parameters (e.g., trying to take a train across oceans), providing elegant multimodal transit suggestions instead.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Smart Itinerary Generation:** Instantly builds a day-by-day travel timeline complete with visual activity markers, detailed transport segments, and departure-time adaptations.
*   **Transit Choice Advisory:** Evaluates and flags geographically impossible transit choices (such as *Train* or *Bus* from Hyderabad to Singapore), suggesting intelligent multimodal itineraries (like "Train to hub + Flight" or "Flight only") with friendly alerts.
*   **Contextual Packing Checklist:** Automatically creates a minimal, tailored packing list matching chosen transit methods, duration, weather forecasts, and destination constraints (such as modest clothing requirements for spiritual or religious visits).
*   **Dynamic Itinerary Modification:** Let users converse with an AI travel agent to adaptively refine, add, or subtract specific travel days and activities.
*   **Complete Budget Auditing:** Provides precise, granular estimations generated in the local currency of your trip destination (e.g., ₹, $, €, £) covering transport, accommodation, food, and activities.
